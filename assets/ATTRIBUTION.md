## About the Liberated Pixel Cup

The [Liberated Pixel Cup](https://lpc.opengameart.org/) was a 2012 game competition focused on creating free (as in freedom) game content. The resulting assets are available under CC-BY-SA 3.0 and/or GPL 3.0 licenses, making them ideal for open-source game projects.

The LPC community continues to contribute compatible assets through [OpenGameArt.org](https://opengameart.org/).
